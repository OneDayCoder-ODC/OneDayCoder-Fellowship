Frequently Asked Questions

📄 What is meant by Internal Evaluation Report?
It’s a formal summary of your performance during the internship, including mentor evaluation based on tasks and learning.
📦 What is a Physical Certificate?
A printed, company-sealed certificate with QR verification and credential ID, delivered to your address.
📚 What is covered in the One Week Training?
Front-end development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, with live sessions and interview preparation kits.
🪪 Will I get a Physical ID Card?
Yes, Premium plan interns receive a printed and branded internship ID card.
✅ Will OneDayCoder verify my internship if my college contacts you?
Yes, our team responds to college or university verifications via email or phone.
🏠 Can I opt for an offline internship?
Yes, we offer hybrid models where you can work remotely with optional offline sessions.
🕒 When will I receive my certificate?
Virtual certificates are delivered in 2–3 days. Physical ones take 7–10 business days.
💰 When do I get the ₹150 referral reward?
After your referred friend successfully enrolls, you’ll receive ₹150 within 3–5 working days.
📨 How do I get access to the custom internship email?
Premium interns are issued a personalized email ID within 48 hours of onboarding.
